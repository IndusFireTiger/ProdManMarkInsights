> **Focus:** Product Marketing · GTM · Growth experimentation. A simulated launch
> campaign for a new learning-app MVP, built to demonstrate launch planning,
> positioning and KPI tracking.

## Objective

Design a go-to-market strategy that boosts early adoption and sustained
engagement for an EdTech MVP — and instrument it so every phase has a clear
success metric.

## The GTM plan

| Phase | Channel | Message | KPI |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-launch | Instagram + email | "30 Days of Story Learning Challenge" | Sign-ups |
| Launch | Parenting groups | "Learn through stories" | App installs |
| Growth | In-app challenges | "Earn badges, unlock stories" | Retention |

## The experiment

An A/B test on the landing-page tagline:

- *"Make Learning a Daily Adventure"* (emotional)
- *"Learn Stories, Build Values"* (functional)

## Result (hypothetical)

~10% week-on-week sign-up growth, with a higher click-through rate on the
**emotional** message.

## Learnings

- **Emotional messaging outperformed functional messaging** for this audience —
  parents buy the feeling before the feature.
- **Parent influencers drive authentic trust** in a way paid reach can't buy.

*This simulation is a small-scale expression of the ideas in
[The GTM Factory](/perspectives/the-gtm-factory) — tiered assets, message
testing and a cadence tied to the launch.*
